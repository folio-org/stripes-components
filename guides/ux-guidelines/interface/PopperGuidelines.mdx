# Popper component

Popper manages anchored overlay positioning for dropdowns, popovers, and other contextual surfaces.

## Quick example

<UxExampleProvider>
  <MiniPopperExample />
</UxExampleProvider>

## Structure

Use a stable anchor element and attach overlays with clear spatial relation to that trigger.

Choose placement rules that prioritize readability and avoid clipping.

## Behavior

Overlays should reposition when viewport constraints change.

When overlays are interactive, clicking outside or toggling trigger state should close them predictably.
