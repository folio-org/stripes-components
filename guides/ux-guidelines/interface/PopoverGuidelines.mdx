# Popover component

Popover reveals contextual content without blocking the current workflow.

## Quick example

<UxExampleProvider>
  <MiniPopoverExample />
</UxExampleProvider>

## Structure

A popover has a trigger element and a small overlay anchored near that trigger.

Overlay placement should adapt to available space and keep context relationship clear.

## Behavior

Selecting the trigger opens the overlay. Selecting again or clicking outside closes it.

Popover content should be concise and contextual, not a replacement for full-page flows.
