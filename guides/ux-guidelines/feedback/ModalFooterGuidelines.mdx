# Modal footer component

Modal footer organizes confirmation and dismissal actions at the end of modal workflows.

## Quick example

<UxExampleProvider>
  <MiniModalFooterExample />
</UxExampleProvider>

## Structure

Place the primary action at the visual end of the footer and keep secondary actions adjacent.

Keep labels short and action-oriented to reduce decision time.

## Behavior

Footer actions should stay pinned while modal content scrolls.

Disable or gate primary actions only when validation or processing requires it.
