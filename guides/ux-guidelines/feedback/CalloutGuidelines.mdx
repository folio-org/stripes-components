# Callout component

A callout is a self-dismissing bar used to give users non-critical information about action status within an app or another app.

## Quick example

<UxExampleProvider>
	<MiniCalloutExample />
</UxExampleProvider>

## Structure

Callouts should include a success or error icon, a short message, and a close icon.

The message should be short and update users on an action that has been performed. Suggested format: `Type of action: Name of content where action occurred`.

## Location and size

Callouts appear at the bottom-right of the screen and should take less than 50% of the screen width on desktop.

## Behavior

Callouts slide from the left when non-critical information needs to be shown.

Information displayed in callouts should be relevant in the short term only.

If users do not click the close icon, callouts should slide down automatically after a period of time: 6 seconds if there are no action links, and 10 seconds for toasts with links.

Timing should stop if users hover over a callout.

If several toasts are needed, they should appear stacked.

## Style

Callouts are rectangles with 6px corner radius and a 1px outline.

Background color can be either green (success) or red (failure), providing visual feedback.
